Vikas Khanna

Vikas Khanna has certainly made a name for himself, not just in India, but even internationally. He is a Michelin star chef and his restaurant Junoon has won Michelin stars for 6 consecutive years! In case you still don’t recognise him, he was the host of the MasterChef India Season 2. A great chef and an equally nice person, his passion for culinary art is enough to make you want to start cooking!
